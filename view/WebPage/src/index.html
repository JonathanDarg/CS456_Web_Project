Our Mission

At Forever Paws, our mission is to provide a safe and loving environment for dogs in need while actively working toward finding them forever homes.

We are committed to rescuing dogs from shelters, providing them with necessary medical care, and offering support to adopters to ensure successful placements. Our goal is to create a community where every dog has the opportunity to experience love, care, and a happy life.

Founded in 2018, Forever Paws began as a small, volunteer-driven effort focused on helping local shelters reduce overcrowding and give abandoned dogs a second chance. What started with a handful of passionate animal advocates has grown into a dedicated adoption center serving hundreds of dogs and families each year. For over seven years, we have worked closely with rescue partners, veterinarians, and community volunteers to improve the lives of animals in need.

Our mission goes beyond adoption. Every dog that enters our program receives comprehensive medical evaluations, vaccinations, and behavioral support to prepare them for a successful transition into a loving home. We believe responsible adoption includes education and ongoing support, which is why we provide resources, training guidance, and post-adoption assistance to families long after adoption day.

Since opening our doors, Forever Paws has successfully rescued and placed more than 1,200 dogs into permanent homes. Our organization has hosted community adoption events, partnered with local schools to promote humane education, and launched foster programs that have saved countless dogs requiring extra care and rehabilitation. These accomplishments reflect the dedication of our volunteers, donors, and adopters who share our vision of a compassionate community where no dog is left behind.

As we continue to grow, our focus remains the same: creating safe pathways from rescue to forever homes while strengthening the bond between people and animals. Every adoption represents a new beginning, and every success story reminds us why our mission matters.

Why Choose Adoption?

Adopting a dog from Forever Paws not only gives a deserving animal a second chance at life but also brings immeasurable joy and companionship into your home. By choosing adoption, you are helping to reduce the number of homeless pets and supporting our efforts to rescue and care for more dogs in need. Adopted dogs often show immense gratitude and love, making them wonderful additions to any family. When you adopt from Forever Paws, you are not just giving a dog a home; you are gaining a loyal friend for life.

Get Involved

There are many ways to get involved with Forever Paws beyond adoption. You can volunteer your time to help care for the dogs, assist with events, or even foster dogs in need of temporary homes. Donations are also greatly apreciated and go directly towards providing food, medical care, and shelter for the dogs we rescue. By supporting Forever Paws, you are making a tangible difference in the lives of countless dogs and helping us continue our mission of rescue and rehoming.

Dogs in Need of Help

Every day, dogs in our communities face abandonment, neglect, or unsafe living conditions. Many of these animals depend on compassionate people to speak up and help them find safety, medical care, and loving homes. If you know of a dog that may need assistance whether they are a stray, in a difficult situation, or simply need to be rehomed you can help us make a difference.

Our organization works closely with volunteers, foster families, and local rescue partners to evaluate each situation carefully. Once a report is submitted, we review the information provided and determine the best way to help, whether that means arranging rescue support, connecting owners with resources, or helping the dog transition into a safe and caring environment. Every submission helps us better understand where help is needed most.

By submitting information about a dog in need, you allow our team and volunteers to review the situation and connect the animal with the right resources. Even small actions can lead to life-changing outcomes for these dogs. Together, we can ensure that more animals receive the care, attention, and second chances they deserve.
