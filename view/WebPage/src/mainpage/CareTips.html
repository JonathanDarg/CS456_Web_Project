Care Tips

When rescueing a dog, it is important to be patient and understanding as they adjust to their new home. Many rescue dogs come from difficult or unknown backgrounds, so they may need time to build trust and feel safe. Creating a calm environment, establishing routines, and providing gentle training can help them settle in. Regular veterinary care, proper nutrition, and lots of positive attention will support both their physical and emotional recovery as they transitio into a loving, permanent home.
